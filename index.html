About

Welcome! I'm Stephen Newby, and I'm a Software Engineer fascinated by what happens under the hood.

My journey into software development has been anything but linear, but it's given me a unique perspective. I'm deeply passionate about low-level software, understanding how foundational code drives efficiency, scalability, and ultimately, business success. My academic background, with dual engineering degrees and a robust mathematical foundation, is where I first honed my problem-solving abilities – skills I now apply to architecting and implementing elegant solutions for complex technical challenges.


What truly sets me apart is my prior 15-year career in the accounting sector. From managing books and training staff to implementing IT solutions and preparing client taxes, I gained invaluable insight into the day-to-day operational realities of businesses. This experience taught me to not just build software, but to truly understand why it's needed and how it can deliver tangible value. I pride myself on bridging the gap between intricate technical execution and overarching strategic business objectives.


When I'm not immersed in code, you'll likely find me out on the disc golf course, channeling my inner performer on stage for karaoke or improv, experimenting with new recipes in the kitchen, staying active, or planning my next adventure. I believe these diverse interests fuel my creativity and offer fresh perspectives for my technical work.
